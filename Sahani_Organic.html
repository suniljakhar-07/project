Our Story

Sahani Organics is a initiatives to provide organic products at a lower price, so that more people will use organic

products and may prevent a number of diseases caused by harmful pesticides used in huge quantity, even, some of the 

banned chemicals for cultivation. Motivated by higher profit, even, the retailers used a number of harmful preservatives

to keep the vegetables and fruits fresh and good look. Unfortunately, urban peoples are not able to differentiate the 

chemical used products and organic products. Hence, we will propagate the real facts about  “fake freshness” of these 

products we consume daily. Hence, it is our commitment   to provide option among the buyers to choose nutritious and

healthy food without the side effects of harmful chemical exposures. Our main motto is not profit, even, we can provide 

organic products at the cost “No profit, no Loss”.
 
At present time, not only the physical health rather than mental health of our nation is gradually turning towards worst side.

Due to use of chemical fertilizers and Pesticides, our environment is also facing problem. The soil, water and even air are

polluted to a great extent and the outcomes of these conditions, manifested in the form of number of diseases. Considering 

these burning issue, we had discussed within our friend circle to do something for our people, specially to provide a platform 

for pesticides and chemical free food. With several meetings and discussion, four person agreed to start organic farming. 

During lockdown period, we are manage to acquire 2 acre of land on lease for a period of eight years and started farming 

from 1st June, 2020. Our farm is situated at Ghulal Village, just 1 km from Nilon Pul, Samrala, Ludhiana  district of

Panjab.

Right, now, we had started agricultural activity, goat farming and poultry (Kadaknath) and in the month of February-March

will start Bee keeping also.
